Advese effect

More significant side effects include stomach ulcers, stomach bleeding, and worsening asthma.[9]
Bleeding risk is greater among those who are older, drink alcohol, take other NSAIDs, or are on other blood thinners.[9]
Aspirin is not recommended in the last part of pregnancy.[9] It is not generally recommended in children with infections because of the risk of Reye syndrome.[9] High doses may result in ringing in the ears.
